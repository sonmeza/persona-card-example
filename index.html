Sarah Jenkins

The "Just-in-Time" Commuter

Sarah lives 20 minutes off-campus and works a part-time job in the mornings. She usually arrives on campus exactly 10 minutes before her first class and cannot afford to spend time circling for parking.

Goals

  • Find an open parking spot in under 5 minutes.
  • Avoid getting parking tickets.
  • Minimize the walking distance to the Engineering Building.

Frustrations

  • Arriving at a "Jefferson" deck only to find it actually full.
  • The app crashing or lagging when she is driving.
  • Unclear signage about permits after 4 PM.
